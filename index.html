FAQ

Answers to questions frequently asked about the Template

How do I manage DRP and Assets purchased through DRP?

Distributions that are reinvested need to be entered as new units in the ETF/Stocks tab manually just as you would a normal ETF/Stock purchase. The key difference being that the brokerage is now $0, so overwrite what's in the brokerage Column and set this to $0. To determine the price and number of units purchased, you will need to confirm this with Sharesight or your Broker statement to be confirm these amounts.

Why do my brokerage amounts always change between 1x and 2x brokerage amounts?

The Brokerage in the Template is based upon the logic that there is always 2x brokerage for your first purchase of a holding (buy/later sell) and then 1x brokerage for all purchases of that holding thereafter (only buy, as you'll likely eventually sell in 1 big batch under one parcel).

How do I use the tool if I'm paid fortnightly?

First off, in the SheetOptions tab set your pay Cycle to fornightly and update your fornightly pay value. This will help the Template understand your monthly pay. When it comes to the Sheet however, all calculations are strictly performed in the month timescale. This is to enable a lot of date functions to work on a standardized timescale. So tracking and statistics will be performed at the monthly time scale.

I get paid irregular amounts, how do I account for that?

2 ways. Either a) Enter in an average figure that you are comfortable with or b) Set your Salary to $0 and enter all your income into the Side Income tab. This was designed for irregular amounts and will automatically feed an averaged value into the rest of the Template.

How do I track with my partner?

You'll either have to combine finances for the purposes of this Template or seperate your Sheets. There is no way for the Template to know what belongs to who :-)

How do I migrate data between sheets?

Please see the Migration Instructions tab in the Template as this mary vary from version to version.
