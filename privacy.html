Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2024

Thank you for using our service and the CompiledSanity spreadsheet. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service and process a payment via PayPal.

The Spreadsheet
We do not collect any personal information from you when you use our spreadsheet. We do not require you to provide us with any personal information, such as your name, email address, or any other identifying information. The Sheet has been designed to be completely self-contained. When you download the Sheet and make a copy, it's a self-enclosed, independent copy hosted by you.

No personally identifying information or sensitive information such as passwords/usernames are needed nor requested. The only inputs required are holdings and quantities - the minimum information needed to determine your net worth & associated performance. These are stored securely and self-enclosed within your sheet. All data is self-hosted and processing is performed locally within your own Google account. However, we may receive certain information through PayPal in order to process the purchase of the sheet. This information may include your name, email address, billing address, and payment details. Please note that we do not store any payment information ourselves; all payment transactions are processed securely through PayPal. See below for more information.

Information We Collect
We do not collect any information from you when you use our spreadsheet. We do not use any tracking technologies, such as cookies or web beacons, to collect information about your usage of the spreadsheet. When you make a payment through PayPal, we may receive certain information from PayPal in order to process your payment. This information may include:
  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your billing address - This is neither requested nor needed. Please opt-out at purchase.

How We Use Your Information
We use the information provided by PayPal solely for the purpose of processing payments for our services. We do not use this information for any other purpose, and we do not share it with any third parties unless required by law.

Security
We take the security of your information seriously and take reasonable measures to protect it. All payment transactions are encrypted and processed securely through PayPal's payment gateway. Even though we do not collect any personal information from you or the spreadsheet, we ask that you take reasonable precautions to protect the spreadsheet from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We recommend that you setup 2FA/MFA and other recommend security measures on your account to protect against unauthorised access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes we make will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Subreddit or via PM. By using the service and/or spreadsheet, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
